_Def 2.1_
A null set $A\subseteq R$  is a set that may be covered by a sequence of intervals of arbitrarily small total length, i.e. given any $\varepsilon > 0$ we can find a sequence $\{I_n: n\geq 1\}$ such that
$$ A\subseteq \bigcup_{n=1}^\infty I_n$$
and $$\sum_{n=1}^\infty l(I_n)<\varepsilon.$$
_Theorem 2.1_
If $(N_n)_{n\geq1}$ is a sequence of null sets, then their union $$N = \bigcup_{n=1}^\infty N_n$$ is also null.
